How It Works

System Architecture

Our application operates through a series of specialized AI interactions, each designed to address specific aspects of disaster preparedness. Here's an overview of the core components:

1. Specialized Instructions

Each tool operates with a specific set of instructions that define its expertise and behavior. For example, the Resource Finder has detailed knowledge about emergency services and community resources. These instruction sets are transparent and available in our GitHub repository.

2. Processing System

When you interact with a tool, your input is processed by OpenAI's GPT-4 model alongside our specialized instructions. This combination ensures responses are both relevant to disaster preparedness and tailored to your specific situation.

3. Data Handling

Our system processes all information in real-time without persistent storage. This means your conversations and uploaded documents are handled temporarily and securely, then completely removed when you end your session.

4. Transparent Development

The entire application is open source and publicly available on GitHub. This includes our codebase, instruction sets, and system architecture, allowing for community review and contribution.

üí° Design Philosophy

Our approach prioritizes transparency and community ownership. By implementing a stateless architecture and open-source development model, we've created a system that can be independently verified, enhanced, and replicated by other communities and organizations working in disaster preparedness.
